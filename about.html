Our Story

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Paris, PAAREES began as a small boutique dedicated to the art of scent. Our founder, inspired by the timeless elegance of French perfumery, sought to create a bridge between heritage and modern luxury.

What started with a handful of signature scents has grown into a global curation of the world's most prestigious perfume houses. Every fragrance in our collection is hand-selected, ensuring that we only offer "Chef-d'oeuvres" (masterpieces) to our clientele.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted destination for olfactory excellence, where every customer finds their unique invisible signature. We aim to redefine the digital fragrance shopping experience through personalized consultation and absolute authenticity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to luxury niche perfumes while maintaining the highest standards of exclusivity. We are committed to sourcing only genuine products and providing a seamless, sophisticated journey from discovery to delivery.
